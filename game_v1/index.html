General playing info:

  • Two starting teams (good and evil)
  • Multiple characters can attack
  • Attacks stay during the course of the game
  • Two attacks against the same player kills that player (there are some ways to dodge)
  • Evil wins by killing Guardian or attacking every good character at least once
  • Good wins by killing Shadow Walker

Starting Characters:

  • Guardian - When you die, good loses. If two attacks occur to you in a single night, you will dodge one. Once during the game, at night (last action), you may pass your guardianship to another alive player (they lose their ability)
  • [D] Vigilante - You may attack a player at night. When you die, this ability passes to a random good character that is still alive, but they lose their ability
  • Homunculus - When you die, choose a player to learn their character (The Shadow Walker and current Guardian both register as N/A)
  • [A] *Twin 1 - You know who Twin 2 is. Once during the game, if you’re both alive, you may attack a player together
  • [A] *Twin 2 - You know who Twin 1 is. Once during the game, if you’re both alive, you may attack a player together
  • **Scarlet Witch - You may kill any one player with a single attack (except the Guardian) but you reveal yourself to everyone after the attack
  • **Shadow Walker - You must attack each night. Evil wins when you have killed the Guardian or attacked every non-evil player AND the serial killer is dead. Instead of attacking, you may kill yourself to transfer your role to another member on your team.
